Introduction

IEEE ComSoc Summer School – Jaffna, Sri Lanka

Envisioning the Future – Pioneering Next Generation Communication Technologies


This IEEE ComSoc School Series event aims to develop a strong foundation in communication technologies while introducing participants to cutting-edge research trends that are reshaping the field. The 5-day summer school will focus on next-generation communication systems with a special emphasis on Quantum Communication, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. Participants will also explore advanced domains and their applications, including Optical Communication Systems, Radar and Imaging, Natural Language Processing, Deep Space Communication, Blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT).

The school is designed for individuals passionate about exploring and deepening their knowledge of communication technologies. Whether you come with a basic understanding or a strong background in telecommunications, computer science, electronics, or related disciplines, this summer school offers invaluable insights and hands-on experiences.

We are proud to host a diverse faculty of leading academics known for their contributions to communication technologies and industry experts with real-world implementation experience. This unique integration of academic excellence and industry relevance ensures a high-impact learning environment.

The comprehensive program includes:
- 20+ hours of ecture sessions
- 10+ hours of practical hands-on training and tutorials
- Panel discussions and networking opportunities

This summer school provides an exceptional platform for undergraduate and postgraduate students to:
- Grasp the state-of-the-art in next-generation communication systems
- Apply modern techniques and tools for practical innovations
- Engage in interdisciplinary discussions and collaborations
- Connect with professionals and researchers for future opportunities

Above all, the IEEE ComSoc Summer School in Jaffna, Sri Lanka, strives to empower participants with the skills and knowledge to contribute to the betterment of Sri Lanka through technological advancement.

Target Group

The target audience for the Summer School on Computational Intelligence includes undergraduate and postgraduate students, as well as enthusiasts eager to expand their knowledge and skills in this rapidly evolving field. Regardless of your background or level of expertise, this program offers a valuable learning experience and an opportunity to engage with experts and peers in the computational intelligence community.

Participation is available for a limited number of ComSoc Student Members from the Sri Lanka region. Membership is not required in order to apply; however, if your application is accepted, you will need to become a ComSoc Student Member in order to register and attend the event.

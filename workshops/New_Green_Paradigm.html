Workshop Session: A New Green Paradigm for Wireless Communication Networks



Prof Dushantha Jayakody

Prof. Dush N. K. Jayakody is a Senior Member of IEEE and a Fellow of IET. He received his Ph.D. in Electronics and Communications Engineering from University College Dublin, Ireland, in 2014, and his MSc in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Eastern Mediterranean University, Turkey. He was ranked among the top 2% of scientists in the world in 2021-2024 according to the Stanford-Elsevier list. In 2023 (for 2019/2020), he received the Sri Lankan Presidential Award for his outstanding research performance, among many other awards and recognitions. In 2025 (for 2021), he received the National Research Council Merit Award for his outstanding research performance, among many other awards and recognitions.
